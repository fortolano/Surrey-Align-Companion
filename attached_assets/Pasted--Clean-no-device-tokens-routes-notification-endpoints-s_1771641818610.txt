  ★ Insight ─────────────────────────────────────
  The Replit agent's approach is pragmatic. Pull-based polling (fetch on app focus/resume) is the right starting point for an internal app with ~80
  users who check it intentionally. Push notifications are a UX enhancement, not a functional requirement — the action-required endpoint already
  surfaces everything the user needs when they open the app. If the team eventually wants background notifications, the server-side
  NotificationController is already in place, and the push infrastructure can be added without touching anything that exists today.
