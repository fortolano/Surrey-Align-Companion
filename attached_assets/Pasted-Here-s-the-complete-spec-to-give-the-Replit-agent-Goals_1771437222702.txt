  Auth: All endpoints require Authorization: Bearer <sa_token> header

  1. List Goals
  - Method: GET
  - URL: /goals
  - Query params (all optional):
    - scope — stake, ward, organization, committee (default: all)
    - status — active, draft, completed, archived, all (default: active)
    - period_id — specific cycle ID (default: current active cycle)
